Our Story

The Beginning

Our journey with Tasty Table began from a deeply personal place. After being diagnosed with an automimmune disease I found myself needing to strictly eat at home. This new lifestyle posed a challenging question every day: "What's for dinner?" It was during this period of adjustment and exploration that the idea for Tasty Table was born. I realized that I wasn't alone in my struggle to find inspiration in the kitchen, and I saw an opportunity to create a solution that could help many others as well.

Our Mission

Our mission at Tasty Table is simple - to make discovering new recipes as easy and enjoyable as possible. We believe that cooking should be a joy, not a chore. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a newbie in the kitchen, we want to provide you with a diverse range of recipes at your fingertips, encouraging you to try new dishes and expand your culinary horizons.

Our Values

At the heart of Tasty Table are our core values. We are committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity in the kitchen. That's why our recipe database includes dishes from a wide range of cuisines, catering to different dietary needs and preferences. We also value simplicity and ease of use, which is why we've designed our search engine to be as intuitive and user-friendly as possible.

Our Journey

Building Tasty Table was a journey filled with challenges and learning opportunities. One major challenge was finding an API that provided a diverse set of meals. After much searching, we discovered the MealDB API, which proved to be an excellent resource. Implementing session storage to pass and render data across different pages was another hurdle we overcame. Despite the hurdles, we persisted, driven by the desire to make home cooking a more enjoyable and accessible experience for all.
